About me

Hey there! It's a pleasure to have you drop by and check out my online portfolio. My name is Nejc, and I come from a small country in the middle of Europe named Slovenia, which has a stunning landscape and rich cloture. I have a strong interest in technology, and can often find myself being curious about new trends.

My programming journey began in 2016 during my high school years. It all started with the fundamentals of Java and the creative power of HTML/CSS. Looking ahead to the present, I've cultivated my programming skills into a thriving ecosystem, now I am able to use over 10 diverse programming languages and frameworks. While I still need to polish and master a lot of my skills, I am always looking forward to learning new things and diving deeper into the world of technology.

For me learning and creating new things was always exciting. When I do things, I try to do them quickly and as efficiently as possible without creating mistakes that will haunt me later down the line. This encourages me to seek opportunities for learning and personal growth, especially from individuals with more experience, even in areas where I may already have some knowledge, as there might always be a better way to accomplish the same task.

When I’m not solving complex problems or brewing code, I like to play video games with friends or by myself. For me playing games is a relaxing experience at which I can always have some fun and a good laugh. Besides being enclosed behind four walls I also like going to the gym and find new adventures in and out of my country, I don’t have particle preference of where to go, usually if somebody has an interesting idea presented to me will most likely happily go with them and gain new experience.

In conclusion, I am a technophile and a willing wanderer who is always looking for a challenge and improvement. My experiences in technology, games and my travels have shaped who I am today. Through this section, I want to share a piece of my world with you.

Feel free to explore my work and don’t hesitate to contact me if you have any questions or just want to chat! I will be happy to talk with you!
